What is the name of the website

Who is the site's target audience

I would say the target audience if for the people who want the college experience as well as a chance of getting a degree for the major they chose or even more.

How is the cite organized

The site is organized in a way that makes the home page look more visually stunning to the audience, showing slideshows of the campus life as well as students learning. It also shows the links on the top left that guides users to where they want to get infomation from.

The CRAP principle

The repitition is shown throughout the website giving similar colors to each section as well as styles and sizes giving a consistent look to the cite that makes it almost addictive to look at.

The Audit score

The accesibility checker shows that the site has a 89% audit score.

Whats the sites effective

The site is very effective I have had no problems with the links at all, I am able to navigate through the site with ease finding what i need as well as looking as the live films of the school life in the home page, showing no signs of lag or any other issues

What is the sites effenciency

I have had no issues getting into the website as well as no problems clicking into links and getting me where i need to go quickly and easily with no complaints i have been able to click what i want with ease without any issue and print everything i would need from the site.

Engagement

The site has a very smooth way of engaging the users to want to explor the website more with its pictures and big fonts as well as colors.
