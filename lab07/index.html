Each color has a different meaning as well as different emotions. Colors can help people express themselves and their thoughts, colors make life feel less empty and more joyful. Creating this table chart helped me understand more about creating tables. It can help you organize data correctly by putting information in rows and columns helping it become more easy to see, teaching you to be more organized.
